Shadows of Fortune

Uncover the secrets of Shadow Falls in this idle survival horror RPG on the blockchain. Fight relentless zombies, gather loot, and equip yourself to survive. Trade rare objects with other survivors, leveraging blockchain technology for secure, lightning-fast transactions at minimal cost, all powered by Sonic.

Shadow of Fortune is a turn-based adventure and survival game set in the mysterious town of Shadow Falls, now plunged into a zombie apocalypse. Choose your path wisely, use resources smartly, and fight bravely against the horrors lurking in the streets of Shadow Falls. As you navigate through this nightmare, you will uncover the darkest secrets of the city and eventually escape... with a big bag of cash!

Your choices and intelligence are the only way to survive in this city. Each survivor is left to his or her own devices, in competition with the others. Your death is often a godsend for the most patient and cunning. So will you be the king of the long night or just a gourmet zombie dish?
