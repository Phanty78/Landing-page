Shadows of Fortune

Shadow of Fortune is a turn-based adventure and survival game set in the mysterious town of Shadow Falls, now plunged into a zombie apocalypse. Choose your path wisely, use resources smartly, and fight bravely against the horrors lurking in the streets of Shadow Falls. As you navigate through this nightmare, you will uncover the darkest secrets of the city and eventually escape... with a big bag of cash!
